This application calculates your Basal Metabolic Rate(BMR), which is how many calories you burn at a resting state in 24 hours. It is the bare minimum amount of calories you could possibly burn.

We then calculate your daily burned calories when you select your activity level and add it to your BMR.
Once you select your goal weight and time frame, we then calculate the amount of extra calories you would have to burn per day and subtract it from your BMR. If you are looking to gain weight, we add it to your BMR.

If your calories happen to fall to an unhealthy amount, we ask you to redefine your goal time frame to a more realistic and healthy one.

We hope you enjoy using our application, and look forward to adding more functionality and improving it over time.
